Our Mission

Every AI chatbot deployment creates a privacy risk. Companies send millions of messages containing PII to OpenAI, Claude, and other LLMs daily - exposing themselves to GDPR fines, HIPAA violations, and data breaches.

Aegis was founded to solve this critical gap in AI security. We believe that organizations shouldn't have to choose between AI innovation and privacy compliance.

Our patent-pending technology provides real-time PII protection that auto-configures in seconds, detects sensitive data with 99.99% accuracy, and processes 35M requests per second - all while maintaining sub-50ms latency.
